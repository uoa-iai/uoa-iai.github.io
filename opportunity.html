Join Us

Shape the future of industrial intelligence with us

We are looking for talented and motivated postgraduate students for our Master of Engineering and Ph.D. programs, as well as Postdoctoral researchers. Our group works on cutting-edge topics spanning industrial communication, artificial intelligence, IoT, and advanced manufacturing — bridging fundamental research with real-world industrial impact.

Our Values

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in research, rigour, and impact.

Creativity

We encourage novel ideas and unconventional thinking to solve hard problems.

Passion

We are driven by genuine curiosity and enthusiasm for our work.

Teamwork

We collaborate openly across disciplines and support each other's growth.

Open Positions

Ph.D.

Doctor of Philosophy

Full-time doctoral research positions in AI, robotics, and industrial systems. Ph.D. candidates are fully supervised and supported to publish internationally and develop independent research expertise.

M.Eng.

Master of Engineering

Research-focused master's positions working alongside doctoral students and staff on active projects. Ideal for candidates looking to deepen technical expertise before pursuing a Ph.D.

Postdoc

Postdoctoral Researcher

Postdoctoral opportunities are available for international applicants with a strong publication record. Postdocs lead independent research threads and mentor graduate students.

Who We're Looking For

Strong candidates typically hold a degree in one of the following disciplines and demonstrate excellent mathematical, programming, and analytic skills.

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Mechatronics Engineering
  • Control and Automation
  • Industrial Engineering
  • Computer Science / AI / Robotics

Funding Opportunities

UoA – China Scholarship Council (CSC)

Available for Ph.D. and postdoctoral applicants from China. Covers tuition fees and provides a living allowance for the duration of study.

University of Auckland Doctoral Scholarship

Awarded to high-achieving doctoral candidates enrolled in approved full-time doctoral programmes at the University of Auckland.

How to Apply

Send an email to Dr. Yuqian Lu with the subject line "Prospective [Position] – [Your Name]" and include the following:

  1. The position and research area you are interested in
  2. A brief summary of your relevant research experience
  3. Information on any external funding or scholarships you hold or are applying for
  4. A detailed CV including GPA(s), research background, and any publications
Only applicants with exceptional academic potential and a strong fit with our current positions will be contacted.
